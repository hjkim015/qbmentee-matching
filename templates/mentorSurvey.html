As a mentor, I agree to fully respect the boundaries I personally establish with my mentee. I will establish these boundaries with a mutual consensus. This includes but is not limited to: respecting the methods of communication we mutually agree to at the beginning of our professional relationship, respecting the time frame of communication established at the beginning of our professional relationship, respecting my mentee's right to privacy and autonomy on their social media platforms, and respecting my mentee's right to cancel or reschedule any form of mentorship including virtual meetings, phone calls and virtual communication.

As a mentor, I agree to guiding my mentee through all application sections in a timely manner. If I cannot finish these, I will clearly communicate this to my mentee.
