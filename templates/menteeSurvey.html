Terms and Conditions

The following are necessary conditions of agreement to move forth with mentorship. We highly value the privacy and mental health of our mentors and mentees. In order to make this possible, we must take the proper precautions to fully protect both mentors and mentees. If these conditions of agreement are broken at any point, mentorship will be terminated immediately and external consequences will be taken if necessary. If you do not agree to all of these conditions of agreement, you put your mentorship at possible risk of rejection.

As a mentee, I agree to completing all of the application portions assigned by my mentor in a timely manner. If I cannot finish these, I will clearly communicate this to my mentor. I will follow my mentor's timeframe, come prepared to every meeting and while I am not required to take all of my mentor's advice, I will respect the wisdom they offer and not attempt to argue with them unless I believe they are presenting an offensive opinion.

As a mentee, I agree to demonstrate appropriate behavior in all of my interactions with my mentor. This includes and is not limited to: refraining from making uncomfortable comments on appearance, refraining from engaging in any form of discriminatory conversation towards a certain demographic, and refraining from beginning any form of romantic or sexual relationship with my mentor.

As a mentee, I agree to any other conditions established by my mentor. By clicking, "I agree to this condition," I am indicating I am fully aware of the conditions in my mentorship, acknowledging the consequences of violating any condition and understanding that I am able to lose this mentorship at any time, given I engage in any inappropriate behavior.
