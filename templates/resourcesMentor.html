College Essays
The College Essay Guy

Has lots of great videos on brainstorming essay ideas that showcase your values and unique experiences. If you're able to start on your essays earlier, you can follow along with the course the website provides! There are also scholarships for low-income students so that you can get 1:1 essay help from a mentor. The program is called matchlighters so make sure to also apply for that!
